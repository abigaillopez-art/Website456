About

Blackpink is a global kpop group who started in August 2016 formed by YG Entertainment. The members of the groups are Jisoo, Jennie and Lisa. Jisoo is singer, songwriter and actress. Jisoo is best known as the lead vocalist and best visual group. Lisa is part of the group but also a soloist, she is a singer, rapper and dancer. She is known for dancing. Then theres Jennie. Jennie is known for being the main rapper of the group. Last but not least is Rosé. Rosé is known for her golden voice. She plays guitar and fashion trend styling
